Welcome to RiseUp

RiseUp Youth Empowerment is a non-profit organisation that empowers young people through skills development, mentorship, and opportunities. This will help the people gain confidence and practical skills that they need to succeed in life so that they can be able to contribute positively to the society. We believe that every student deserves access to the tools, knowledge, and support needed to succeed academically, personally and professionally. We work with students from different backgrounds to help them develop the confidence, knowledge and life skills needed to thrive in today's fast changing world. Our organization is built on the belief that empowered students create empowered communities and a brighter future for tomorrow.
