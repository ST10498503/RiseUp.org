Our History

RiseUp Youth Empowerment was founded in 2020 by a group of young graduates that were very passionate and they recpgnised the growing challenges faced by youth in underpriviledged communities. The organisation was created with the aim of bridging the gap between education and employment and to support people who lack access to opportunities.

Since the founding, this campaign has worked with local communities to provide support, mentorship, and practical skills training. Over the past years, the organisation has positively impacted many young individuals by helping them gain confidence, develop new skills and prepare for the future.

Our Vision

Our Vision in this organisation is to build a future where all young people are empowered to succeed regardless of their background, they must receive equal access to opportunities that seem impossible to them, contribute positively to the society. We strive to create a generation of confident, skilled and motivated individuals. We envision empowered young people who are confident, skilled, career-ready, socially responsible, and capable of making meaningful contributions to their communities and society.

Our Mission

Our mission is to equip youth with the skills and knowledge they need to succeed. We strive to create a positive and supportive environment where students can learn, grow, and build confidence, and prepare themselves for future opportunities in education, employment, entrepreneurship, and leadership

Our Values

  • Empowerment

    • We believe in helping students discover their strengths, build confidence, and take control of their future.

  • Growth

    • We are intended to make the community grow and make the youth have industrial comfort

  • Community

    • We want to be a trustworthy organization that gives back to the community and gain the communities trust by delivering their needs

  • Education

    • We promote lifelong learning and believe education is one of the most powerful tools for change and for development

  • Innovation
